Massachusetts General Hospital announced that its Healey Center for ALS would be including Eli Lilly's oral SARM1 inhibitor LY3873862 in their ALS Platform Trial to see if it is safe and if it prevents or delays ALS progression. If the trial shows good results, it would represent a significant step forward in disease-modifying therapies for ALS. It began enrollment in late 2025.
