VHB937 is a [TREM2](https://www.axonome.org/trem-2) agonist that works by stabilizing the protein with the hope that the immune cells that express it (microglia) will be more active against amyloid plaques. This phase II clinical trial is to assess the safety and efficacy of VHB937. Hopefully, the patients who receive it will have improved memory, be more functional day-to-day, and have better biomarkers than the placebo group. The trial is now recruiting — [see if you qualify](https://www.novartis.com/clinicaltrials/study/nct07094516#trial-eligibility).
